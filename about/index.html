About Me

Howdy! I'm Andrew, I'm from King of Prussia, PA, and I'm 19 years old. I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at Lehigh University's College of Engineering, as well as a B.A. in Music/Jazz in the College of Arts and Sciences. I am also planning on working towards a minor in Graphic Design.

My passion in CS is front end development, although I haven't spent too much time working on it, other than a project I worked on called Liquick, which you can check out below. I am familiar with programming in Java, HTML, C, and C++, so I am comfortable with backend work as well.

As fas ar music goes, I'm studying jazz saxophone with Dave Riekenberg, and perform in Lehigh's Jazz Ensemble (tenor sax) and Tim Harrison's Jazz Combo (baritone sax). Beyond jazz, I am also interested in classical study, I am in Lehigh's Wind Ensemble and Clarinet Choir on Contra-Alto Clarinet, and am a founding member of the university's new Sax Quartet, where I manage the group, choose music, and play baritone sax. I'm also a proud member of the Marching 97!
